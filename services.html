Tech Care 🧹

Printer on the fritz? Network giving you grief?

Give us a call or use the contact form and we'll respond faster than your ISP will.

Add a software optimization to your clean for $25 and get a deal the big box stores can't touch.

We offer discounts for clients who book recurring maintenance services! Reach out to get a schedule set and we'll get whatever tech you've got running cool and fast year round.

We'll also clean any exterior aesthetic messes though we cannot refinish damaged material.

Dust hurts performance, neglect kills machines.
A regular clean and some fresh paste goes a long way.
PC Clean (Basic)
Dust removal, fan blade wipedown, and temp check. Keeps things running at normal temperatures, and is recommended to do 2-4 times annually depending on environment.
Complex setups and most laptops will see a higher charge.
$35
PC Clean (Advanced)
A full deep clean. Everything outlined in the basic clean including fresh thermal paste, heatsink fin cleaning, PCIe slot clean, and a full health check.
Recommended once annually, also for machines that have been neglected or noticeably underperforming.
Complex setups and most laptops will see a higher charge.
$60
