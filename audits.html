Network Assessments

Network
Audits

Every cable, port, and device tells a story.
We'll document it all - identifying faults, risks, and inefficiencies before they become outages or data loss.

⚡
Small hardware issues can lead to major loss. Be it time, money, or your data, most network faults are invisible until they cause a problem. A network audit finds them before they become a headache.
The Process

Three Steps to Risk Mitigation.

A structured, documented assessment of your network. We test, inspect, and deliver a full written report - everything you need to understand the true state of your infrastructure.

1
The Test
  • Every network run checked end to end
  • Cables identified, mapped and labelled
  • Faults, drops, and inconsistencies all logged
  • Shield integrity verified where applicable
2
The Inspect
  • All active hardware physically inspected
  • Temperatures, port activity, and device conditions documented
  • Everything inventoried and accounted for
3
The Report
  • All findings compiled into a single report
  • Severity-rated - critical risks flagged clearly
  • Includes all test results and network mapping
